Our Vision

Plasma Mobile aims to become a complete and open software system for mobile devices.
It is designed to give privacy-aware users back control over their information and communication.
Plasma Mobile takes a pragmatic approach and is inclusive to 3rd party software, allowing the user to choose which applications and services to use, while providing a seamless experience across multiple devices.
Plasma Mobile implements open standards and is developed in a transparent process that is open for anyone to participate in.
